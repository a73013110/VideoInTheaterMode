feature 1, Situation 1 One video in the webpage

1. Open a webpage, for example: Youtube.

2. Click the chrome exetension button or press shortcut key: Ctrl+Shift+F to turn on the theater mode.

3. You will see the video which fill the wabpage.

feature 1, Situation 2 Multi-video in the webpage

1. Open a webpage(some would contain many invisible video), for example: Facebook.

2. Click the chrome exetension button or press shortcut key: Ctrl+Shift+F to turn on the theater mode.

3. It will show the message and just click confirm to select the video.

4. "move the mouse into" the video.

5. You'll see the red transparent panel cover on the video.

6. Just click the red transparent panel and it will turn that video in theater mode.

feature 2 Turn Video to 21:9

1. Turn the video in theater mode.

2. Click the full screen button.

3. You'll see the video turn in full screen with 21:9 ratio
